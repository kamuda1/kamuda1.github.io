About Mark

I'm a PhD candidate in the Advanced Reactor and Fuel Cycle group at the Department of Nuclear, Plasma, and Radiological Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research focuses on applying machine learning to pattern recognition problems in nuclear security and nuclear treaty verification. As a part of my research, I am developing annsa, a Python package for quick gamma-ray spectroscopy datasets prototyping and deep learning using TensorFlow. My interests include advances in deep learning and generative art.
